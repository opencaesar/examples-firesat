dc:description

[=AggregatedElement=] is the domain and rage of the [=aggregates=] property. Aggregation is a homeomeric relation that is potentially many-to-many. Concepts that may participate in such aggregations should specialize [=AggregatedElement=] and apply appropriate range restrictions to [=aggregates=] and [=isAggregatedIn=].
