Class base:ContainedElement

dc:description

[=ContainedElement=] is the range of the [=contains=] property. Containment is a homeomeric one-to-many relation. Concepts that may participate in such as the contained in such relations should specialize [=ContainedElement=] and apply appropriate range restrictions to [=isContainedIn=].
